Classroom Exercises

Classroom exercises are quick movements that combat the effects of prolonged sitting. Simple stretches like shoulder rolls, leg stretches, and desk twists require no equipment and can be done during short breaks. These activities improve circulation, reduce stiffness, and boost concentration, helping students re-engage with lessons more effectively. Regular movement breaks also alleviate physical discomfort that can distract students during class.
